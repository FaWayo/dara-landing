# Weara Website Goal

---

## Primary Goal

Get visitors to join the waitlist.

One action. One input. One button.

---

## After visiting, the user should feel:

**Buyer:** "This was made for me. Nobody has built this for us before. I need to be first."

**Seller:** "These people understand my problem. This will bring me real customers. I'm in."

---

## The Single Action

Enter email → Join waitlist.

---

## Main Message (buyer)

Your wardrobe. The market around you. One styling brain.
Finally built for how African women actually dress.

## Main Message (seller)

Your pieces, in front of the right women. Every day.
Not just when your story is live.

---

## Personality

- Editorial — feels like a magazine, not a SaaS product
- Stylish — the design itself is the proof of concept
- Warm — talks to a real woman, not a user persona
- African — Kantamanto, aso-ebi, owambe are not edge cases
- Modern — could live in a Lagos creative studio or a London fashion flat

---

## What must NOT appear on this page

- Feature lists for things not yet built
- Startup jargon ("seamless", "leverage", "ecosystem")
- Advertising as a value prop
- Generic e-commerce language
- Anything that sounds AI-generated with no cultural grounding
