# Hero Inspiration

## References in this folder
- Shop_Web_7.png — floating product collage hero. Items scattered at
  different depths around a central CTA. Action happening in the hero,
  not a static image. Use this energy for Weara's hero: outfit pieces
  floating around the headline, not a flat banner.

## Direction
- Dark background (#2B2D42)
- Large editorial serif headline, Ghost White
- Floating clothing/outfit cards animated with Framer Motion
- Cards drift in subtly on load — not bouncing, not infinite loop
- Single red CTA button centered below headline
- Mobile: cards stack behind text, headline dominates

## What to avoid
- Full-bleed photo with text overlay (too generic)
- Static hero with no movement
- Anything that looks like a fashion e-commerce banner
