# How It Works Inspiration

## Direction
Four steps. Staggered entrance on scroll. One step revealed at a time.

Step 1 — Upload your wardrobe
Your clothes, tagged and organised. Finally.

Step 2 — Bridge the inspiration gap
Connect your Pinterest boards and saved posts.
Weara matches saved inspiration to pieces you own
or pieces from real sellers near you.
No more saving outfits you can never recreate.

Step 3 — Meet your style avatar
See outfits on a virtual figure styled to your body,
skin tone, and hair — before you leave the house.
Reference: Synthesia_web_65.png for the avatar UI concept.
A person. Clean background. Full or half body.
Styled in the outfit being suggested.

Step 4 — Discover sellers the algorithm hides
Kantamanto pieces and African brand drops shown inside
your outfit — not buried under accounts with bigger followings.

## Layout
- Alternating left/right on desktop
- Stacked on mobile
- Each step fades up on scroll
- No cards. Editorial text + image pairs.

## Avatar UI note
The avatar is not a cartoon. It is a rendered figure.
Skin tone, hair, and body shape are hers.
Reference Synthesia's avatar rendering style —
real-feeling, not illustrated.
