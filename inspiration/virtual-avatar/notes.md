# Virtual Style Avatar Inspiration

## Reference in this folder
- Synthesia_web_65.png — a digitally rendered person, full/half body,
  clean neutral background, realistic rendering style.
  This is the visual reference for how the Weara avatar should feel.
  Not a cartoon. Not an illustration. A figure that reads as real.

## What the avatar does in Weara
She uploads her wardrobe. She tells Weara her body shape,
skin tone, and hair. Weara renders a virtual figure that looks like her
and styles it in the outfit being suggested.
She sees the outfit on herself before she leaves the house.

## How to show it on the landing page
- In the How It Works section, Step 3
- Show a clean UI mockup: avatar figure on the left,
  outfit breakdown on the right
- The figure should read as a Black or African woman
- Background: Space Cadet (#2B2D42) or near-black
- The outfit on the avatar uses real pieces from her wardrobe
  + a suggested seller piece

## What to avoid
- Cartoon avatars or illustrated figures
- Generic mannequins
- Western beauty standard defaults
- Anything that does not look like the Weara user
