# Problem Section Inspiration

## The three beats to show visually

Beat 1 — The Pinterest gap
Visual idea: a Pinterest board on the left,
a wardrobe on the right, a broken line between them.
Or: a mood board collage that feels aspirational but distant.

Beat 2 — The algorithm problem
Visual idea: an Instagram/Facebook feed showing only
high-follower accounts. Small seller buried below the fold.
Or: a scroll metaphor — endless, exhausting, fruitless.

Beat 3 — The disappearing inventory
Visual idea: an Instagram story frame with a countdown.
24h timer. Item there, then gone.

## Layout direction
- Dark background, Ghost White text
- Large editorial statements, not bullet points
- Each beat is a full-width text moment
- Minimal imagery — let the copy carry the weight here
- Subtle fade-up entrance per beat on scroll

## Tone
Empathetic. Specific. Not dramatic.
She already knows this pain — we are just naming it precisely.
